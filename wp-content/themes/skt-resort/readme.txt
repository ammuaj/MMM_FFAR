== Description ==

SKT Resort is lightweight flexible and extendable with easy to use Elementor based homepage sections. Can be used for hotel, lodge, home stay, motel, guest house, holiday home, inn, dormitory, bed and breakfast, villa, cottage, accommodation, farmhouse, service apartment, rooms, reservation, booking, cuisine, restaurants, eateries, street food bloggers, travel adventures diary, coffee tea shops, cafe and hospitality business like oyo, airbnb.
